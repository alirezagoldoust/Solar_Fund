The SOLAR FUND ™

SOLAR FUND is a hospitality, cultural and events brand known for high-end experiences, sharp execution, and strong brand identity, under the alias the boogie series ™. since 2021, it has produced and hosted international events, activated standoutvenues, and cultivated its own community through music, design, and curation. with a focus on detail, atmosphere, and brand partnerships, solar fund has built areputation for delivering refined, experience-driven hospitality grounded incultural edge and musical relevance.

Venue Concepts & Projects

Kelari | Geneva

Solar Fund activated Kelari—transforming an underused space nestled beneath a Greek restaurant into a standout speakeasy club. Leading the creative direction, the team shaped a clear identity with refined music programming and a Void sound system[cite: 60, 61].

HIFI | Gstaad

Developing HIFI within a five-star hotel in Gstaad, reviving a historic club space with a contemporary vision. This venue creates a refined late-night destination for the international crowd that defines the resort each winter[cite: 65, 66].
